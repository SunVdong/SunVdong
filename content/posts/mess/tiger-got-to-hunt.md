Tiger got to hunt, bird got to fly;

Man got to sit and wonder ‘why, why, why?’

Tiger got to sleep, bird got to land;

Man got to tell himself he understand.

— Kurt Vonnegut


> 翻译： 
>
> 虎猎鸟飞人疑惑：为啥为啥为啥捏？
> 
> 虎息鸟憩人自语：好呗算我懂了呗。
